# Dossier Models And Guidance

Working file created 2026-06-17.

Purpose: collect examples of strong promotion materials and practical guidance about building effective dossiers. This file should help shape the case, not become a generic advice archive.

## What To Collect

Good promotion statements, if available.

Good public/digital scholarship dossier examples, if available.

Guidance on effective promotion files from ASU, The College, SHPRS, AHA, NCPH, MLA, ACLS, or peer institutions.

Examples of how candidates present public scholarship, digital projects, collaborative work, oral history, grants, public impact, and evidence that cannot fit inside the formal dossier.

## What To Extract

Strong openings and case frames.

Effective ways of connecting evidence to criteria.

Good methods for presenting digital/public scholarship without defensiveness.

Ways to make public impact legible without overclaiming.

Ways to handle supplemental evidence, public links, appendices, and reviewer-facing evidence pages.
