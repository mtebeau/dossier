# Dossier

Dossier gathers writing, publications, talks, and project records across my work as an urban, public, and digital historian.

It serves as a public-facing record of scholarship, collaboration, and historical work developed across research, teaching, and public humanities practice. Alongside project links, it points to books, articles, essays, professional profiles, and selected institutional affiliations.

My work has included more than two dozen digital humanities, oral history, and public history projects. Recent publications include *A Handbook of Digital Public History*, co-edited with Serge Noiret and Gerben Zaagsma. In Fall 2022, I served as the first Public Historian in Residence at the Centre for Contemporary and Digital History at the University of Luxembourg.
